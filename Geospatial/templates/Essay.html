Data Science Capstone Resource Allocation by Milwaukee Police Department Introduction: This paper examines the use of resources by Milwaukee Police Department and provides a model to help allocate the limited resources in a more efficient way. This paper focuses on the geospatial spread of the calls, the effectiveness of the ShotSpotter program, and response time to analyze the current resource allocation strategy of the MPD. The model predicts the number of calls based on the time of day and the location so that the MPD can allocate more resources to places with a probability of a higher number of calls.
